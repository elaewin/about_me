About

Erica was born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, and has lived long enough in the South and New England to know that Seattle is the place for her.

She's been an editor, anthropologist, professional herder of graduate students, and is a formally trained hand bookbinder. These days she's developing and honing her programming skills, headed towards work as a software developer.

Her current favorite dummy text generator is Bacon Ipsum.
